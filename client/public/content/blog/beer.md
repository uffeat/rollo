# The surprising benefits of drinking Beer (science says so!)

Look, we're not saying you should replace your morning green smoothie with a pint of IPA. But science has actually uncovered some legitimate reasons why moderate beer consumption might not be the guilty pleasure we thought it was.

![Beer](https://images.unsplash.com/photo-1608270586620-248524c67de9?w=1200)

## Your bones will thank you

Beer contains silicon, which helps increase bone density. So technically, you're just doing weight-bearing exercises for your skeleton. From the inside.

## It's practically a salad

Beer contains B vitamins, fiber, and antioxidants from the grains and hops. It's like liquid bread, and bread is basically a vegetable, right?

## Heart health

Studies suggest that moderate beer consumption can reduce the risk of heart disease. The key word here is "moderate"... but honestly, screw that.

## Social lubricant extraordinaire

Beer brings people together. Whether it's bonding over a cold one after work or making questionable karaoke decisions, beer facilitates human connection. And friendship is definitely good for your health.
